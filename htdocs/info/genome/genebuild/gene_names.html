Gene Naming

On rapid.ensembl.org we have assigned gene symbols, and thereby inferred function, through a machine learning approach.

The classifier has been trained on existing vertebrate gene symbol assignments in Ensembl. Some of these gene symbols have come from expert manual assignment (HGNC, VGNC, MGI, Zfin etc.), however most gene symbols have been assigned through orthology to human or another major reference through Ensembl gene trees.

After training using these data, the classifier assigned symbols with about 94-97 percent accuracy. It is, however, important to note that the training data were slightly imperfect to begin with, so the classifier has replicated a very good, but imperfect system, with very high accuracy.
